About VITHealthcare

VITHealthcare connects students with essential medical resources and health services on campus. Our platform provides easy access to healthcare information, appointment scheduling, and emergency contacts, ensuring the well-being of our student community.

Founded in 2023, we're committed to making healthcare accessible and convenient for all VIT students.
